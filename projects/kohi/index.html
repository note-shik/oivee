Our Story

Born from a love
of the craft

Kōhi began with a simple belief: great coffee deserves a great space. We source beans directly from farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — roasting in small batches to bring out flavors you won't find anywhere else.

Every detail in our house — from the hand-thrown ceramics to the playlist — is curated to slow you down, just enough.
